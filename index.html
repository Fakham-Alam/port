HASHEEM

ABOUT

 Hasheem bohot zyada besabab aur sust aadmi hai. Aksar kaam adhora chor deta hai aur ahem zimmedariyon ko taal deta hai. Uski be-tarteeb aur susti se logon ko aksar pareshani hoti hai. Uski khaas baat ye hai ke woh buland aur baar baar pait ki gasen nikalne ki khaasiyat rakhta hai, jis se uske aas paas ke logon ko pareshani hoti hai. Hamare koshishon ke bawajood usko hamesha mohim ki zarurat hoti hai, lekin woh focus banaye rakhne aur kaamon ko waqt par mukammal karne mein musalsal mushkilat ka samna karta hai.

AIK YADGAR WAQIAH

Aik din, mere bhai Hasheem ko kaafi anokha waqiya guzra. Aglay din meri roza khushai thi , aur hum sab ko thatta jane ki tayyariyan karni thi. Lekin raat Hasheem ke liye kafi mushkil guzri, jab unhe shadeed khujli aur chunno ka samna tha. Unho ne apni takleef ko kam karne ke liye mukhtalif tareeqon ko azmaaya, cotton bud se khujate hue se le kar kaghaz ke gole ragarne tak. Ek waqt par, toothpick karne tak puhunch gaye the lekin acha hoa keh hamne dekhliya warna bohat bura hojata. Agle din ham thatta gae hasheem ne nay ready mate kurta pehna hoa tha aur usmain wahan jakar urine kardiya aur phir ba halat e majboori unki shalwar kaaton par sukaani pari aur is tarah din ka ikhtetam hoa
